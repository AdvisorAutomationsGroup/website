FAQ

Questions, answered.

What exactly does Advisor Automations Group do?▾

We implement and automate the tech stack for independent financial advisors and small professional firms. That includes CRM setup, workflow automation, prospect systems, and ongoing monthly support.

Which CRMs and tools do you work with?▾

We primarily work with Wealthbox, Redtail, and GoHighLevel, plus Zapier for integrations between your tools. If you use a different platform, reach out and chances are we can help.

How long does implementation take?▾

Most practices are fully set up within a few weeks depending on complexity. We work efficiently, on-site where needed, and keep you updated throughout the process.

What does ongoing support look like?▾

After implementation we stay on as your monthly tech partner. We handle updates, build new automations, and make sure your systems keep pace with your practice.

How do I get started?▾

Book an intro call. We will walk through your practice, understand what you need, and put together a plan. No pressure, just a conversation.
