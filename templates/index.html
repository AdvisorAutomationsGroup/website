FAQ

Questions, answered.

What exactly does Advisor Automations Group do?▾

We implement and automate the tech stack for independent financial advisors and small professional firms. That includes CRM setup, workflow automation, prospect campaigns, and support when you want it.

Which CRMs and tools do you work with?▾

We commonly work with Wealthbox, Redtail, and GoHighLevel, plus Zapier for integrations between your tools. If you use a different platform, reach out and chances are we can help.

How long does implementation take?▾

Most practices are fully set up within a few weeks depending on complexity. We work efficiently and keep you updated throughout the process.

What does ongoing support look like?▾

After implementation, we can stay on as your monthly tech partner. We handle updates, build new automations, and make sure your systems keep pace with your practice. It's optional, not automatic.

How do I get started?▾

Book a free tech audit. We will walk through your practice, understand what you need, and put together a plan. No pressure, just a conversation.
